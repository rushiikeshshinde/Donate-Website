Who are we?

In our charity, we provide help to people in need and the environment.
People impacted of poverty, starvation, natural disaster and war are some of the people we help every day. Through our help we encourage them to dream of a better life. We support, financially and physically, human rights for people of all ages around the world.
Nature is our home and we need to protect it from ecological disaster. Planting trees, recycling, picking up litter, protecting wildlife and going-extinct animals, reduce water and air polution, are only some of our actions to heal the environment.
